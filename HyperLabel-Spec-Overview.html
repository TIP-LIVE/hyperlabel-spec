Vision 2030

Our Vision: Become the global standard for transparent cargo tracking, eliminating "black holes" in logistics. By 2030, enable real-time visibility for any shipment, anywhere, with predictive intelligence for delays and route deviations.

Problem & Solution

Problem: Black Holes

Cargo goes dark during transit (flights, ocean, remote areas) with no visibility

Problem: Fragmentation

Different carriers = different tracking systems, no unified view for customers

Problem: Reactive

Users discover delays after they happen, not before‚Äîcan't plan ahead

Our Solution

Offline data storage + single interface + proactive alerts = complete visibility

Full User Journey

1

Buy Label Consignee

Consignee (buyer) purchases label from website

2

Enter Final Destination Consignee

Consignee enters their own address (where cargo will be delivered)

3

Share Link with Forwarder Consignee

Consignee sends a link to the forwarder to complete origin address

4

Enter Origin Address Forwarder

Forwarder clicks link, enters their address (cargo origin) ‚Üí label ships to them

5

Receive Label Forwarder

Forwarder receives physical label with quick-start guide

6

Scan QR & Link to Cargo Forwarder

Forwarder scans QR ‚Üí enters cargo details ‚Üí attaches label to shipment

7

Transit & Track Shipper

Cargo moves through carriers, label transmits location every 15-30 min

8

Delivered Consignee

Cargo arrives at consignee, notification sent, tracking complete

Key Insight: The Consignee (buyer) may not know the forwarder's exact address. They share a link so the forwarder can enter their own origin address. This triggers label shipment to the forwarder.

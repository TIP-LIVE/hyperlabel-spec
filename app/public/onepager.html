The Problem

$15–25 billion is lost annually to cargo theft, delays, and misrouted shipments globally. Yet most tracking solutions cost $70–500 per device, require complex setup, and lock customers into carrier-specific platforms.

The result: SMB shippers have limited visibility into their cargo—especially during flights, ocean transit, and handoffs between carriers.

47% of supply chain leaders cite real-time visibility as their #1 investment priority.

Sources: BSI & TT Club Cargo Theft Report 2023; Gartner Supply Chain Survey 2023

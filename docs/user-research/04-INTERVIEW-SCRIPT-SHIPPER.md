# Interview Script — Shipper / Logistics Provider (3PL, Carrier)

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Target:** 2-3 interviews
**Focus:** Industry validation, operational perspective, partnership potential

---

## Before the Call

- [ ] Confirm recording consent
- [ ] Have TIP concept ready to share
- [ ] Open this script + note-taking template

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)

> "Thanks for your time. I'm Denys, building a new cargo tracking product. I'm speaking with logistics professionals to understand the industry perspective. Confidential, no wrong answers. OK to record?"

- Confirm recording consent
- "Tell me about your role and what your company does."

---

## 2. Background (5 min)

- What modes of transport does your company operate? (ocean, air, trucking, multi-modal)
- What types of cargo do you handle?
- How many shipments per month?
- What's your geographic coverage?

---

## 3. Current Tracking Capabilities (15 min)

> "I'd like to understand what visibility you provide to your clients today."

- What tracking systems do you use internally?
- What visibility do you give your clients? (portal, API, manual updates)
- Do your clients ask for more visibility than you can provide?
- Do you use any IoT devices or tracking hardware on cargo today?
- What data do your clients value most? (location, ETA, temperature, proof of delivery)

**Probes:**
- How much do you invest in tracking technology per year?
- Have you evaluated any third-party tracking devices?
- What held you back from adopting IoT tracking?

---

## 4. Industry Perspective (10 min)

- How is demand for cargo visibility changing in your industry?
- Are your competitors offering better tracking? What do they do?
- Do you see disposable IoT labels as a realistic product category?
- What concerns would you have about a SIM-based label attached to cargo?
  - Regulatory? Battery safety? Customs?
- Is cell-tower accuracy (city-level) useful in your operations, or do you need GPS precision?

**Probes:**
- Would you see this as a threat to your own tracking offering, or complementary?
- Are there trade lanes or cargo types where this would be especially valuable?
- Any concerns about labels going through X-ray, customs, or extreme environments?

---

## 5. Solution Reaction (10 min)

**[Share screen — TIP concept]**

> "A disposable label with a SIM, activated by QR scan, reports location via cell towers for the journey duration. The buyer sticks it on at origin and watches a dashboard."

- Initial reaction?
- Would this work operationally on cargo you handle?
- What problems do you foresee?
- Would you want to integrate this into your systems, or keep it separate?
- Would you co-sell this with your logistics services?

**Probes:**
- What if a client showed up with a labeled box — any issues at your end?
- Would you want bulk pricing to offer this to all your clients?

---

## 6. Wrap-Up (5 min)

- What did I miss? What should I be thinking about?
- Would you be open to a pilot — tracking one real shipment?
- Can you connect me with anyone in your network who imports goods and struggles with visibility?

> "Really appreciate your time and industry insight. I'll follow up with a thank-you shortly."

- [ ] Note pilot interest (1-5)
- [ ] Note partnership potential
- [ ] Note referrals

# Interview Script — Consignee (Buyer / Importer)

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Target:** 8-10 interviews
**Focus:** Pain points, willingness to pay, feature priorities

---

## Before the Call

- [ ] Confirm recording consent was given in scheduling email
- [ ] Have TIP concept slides/mockups ready to share screen
- [ ] Open this script + note-taking template
- [ ] Test Zoom/recording

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)

> "Thanks for making time for this. I'm Denys, working on a new product in the cargo tracking space. This is a research conversation — there are no right or wrong answers, and I genuinely want to hear about your real experience, even if it's critical. Everything you share stays confidential and won't be attributed to you by name. Is it OK if I record this for my notes? The recording won't be shared outside our team."

- Confirm recording consent verbally
- "To start, could you tell me your name, role, and a bit about what your company does?"

---

## 2. Background & Context (5 min)

- What's your role day-to-day?
- What types of goods do you import, and from where?
- Roughly how many international shipments do you handle per month?
- What transport modes do you use most? (ocean, air, road)

**Probes:**
- How long have you been in this role / industry?
- Is your company growing its import volume, or is it stable?

---

## 3. Current Tracking Workflow (15 min)

> "I'd love to understand how you track a shipment today, from the moment it leaves the supplier to when it arrives at your door."

- Walk me through what happens after you place an order with your supplier.
- At what point do you first get a tracking number or shipment reference?
- What tools or systems do you use to check where cargo is? (carrier portal, forwarder emails, WhatsApp, spreadsheet, ERP)
- How often do you check the status of a shipment in transit?
- Who else in your team needs to know where a shipment is?

**Probes:**
- Do you have a single dashboard, or do you switch between multiple tools?
- How reliable are the updates you get from your forwarder / carrier?
- How much time per week would you estimate you spend on shipment tracking?
- Do you pay specifically for any tracking service today? How much?

---

## 4. Pain Points & Failures (10 min)

> "Now I want to talk about when things go wrong."

- What's your biggest frustration with tracking international shipments?
- Can you tell me about a specific time when lack of visibility cost you money or caused a problem?
  - What happened?
  - How did you find out?
  - What was the financial impact?
- How do your own customers react when you can't give them an ETA?
- Have you ever had cargo lost, damaged, or significantly delayed where earlier visibility would have made a difference?

**Probes:**
- How often does this kind of thing happen — once a month, once a quarter?
- What's the typical cost when it goes wrong? (penalties, emergency air freight, lost customer)
- Is there a point in the journey where you feel most "blind"?

---

## 5. Solution Exploration (10 min)

> "I'd like to show you a concept we're working on and get your honest reaction."

**[Share screen — show TIP concept: label photo, tracking dashboard mockup, map view]**

> "The idea is a small disposable label with a built-in SIM. You stick it on your cargo at origin, and it reports its location throughout the journey via cell towers — no GPS, no charging, no setup. You'd see it on a dashboard like this."

- What's your first reaction?
- Would this solve the problem you described earlier?
- What questions do you have?
- What would be a must-have feature for you?
- What's missing that would make this a no-brainer?
- The accuracy is city/district level, not street level. Is that useful, or do you need more precision?

**Probes:**
- Would you want your customers to see this dashboard too?
- Would you use this for every shipment, or only high-value ones?
- How would this compare to what your forwarder already provides?

---

## 6. Pricing & Purchase Decision (5 min)

- If this existed today, what would you expect to pay per label, per shipment?
- Our target price is $20-25 per label. How does that feel?
  - Too much? What would be the right price?
  - Fair? Would you buy it right now for your next shipment?
- Who in your company would approve this purchase?
- Would you expense it per shipment, or would it need a budget line?
- What would prevent you from buying this?

**Probes:**
- Would you rather buy labels individually or in bulk packs?
- Would a subscription model (monthly fee + cheaper labels) be more attractive?
- At what shipment value does $20-25 tracking become a no-brainer?

---

## 7. Wrap-Up (5 min)

- Is there anything I didn't ask that you think I should know?
- If we launch a pilot in the next few months, would you be interested in trying it with a real shipment?
- Do you know anyone else — another importer or a forwarder — who might be willing to talk with me?

> "This was incredibly helpful. I'll send you a thank-you gift card within 24 hours. If anything else comes to mind, feel free to email me. Thanks again!"

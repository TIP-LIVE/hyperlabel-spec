---

## Screening Questions (use before scheduling)

Ask these via email, LinkedIn DM, or a short Typeform to qualify candidates.

### Consignee Screener
1. Do you import physical goods internationally? (Yes → continue)
2. Roughly how many international shipments do you handle per month? (Need: 10+)
3. What's your role? (Need: Procurement, Supply Chain, Operations, or similar)
4. How many employees does your company have? (Need: 10-500)
5. What industry are you in? (Prefer: electronics, manufacturing, e-commerce)
6. Are you based in the US or UK? (Prefer: yes)

### Forwarder Screener
1. Does your company provide international freight forwarding? (Yes → continue)
2. Roughly how many shipments does your team manage per month? (Need: 50+)
3. What's your role? (Need: Operations Manager, Account Manager, or similar)
4. Are you based in the US, UK, or EU? (Prefer: yes)

### Shipper/Logistics Screener
1. Does your company move cargo internationally (3PL, carrier, or logistics)? (Yes → continue)
2. What transport modes? (Need: air, ocean, or trucking)
3. What's your role? (Need: Operations, Fleet Manager, or similar)

## Cold Email (no prior connection)

**Subject:** Would love your perspective on cargo tracking (£30 gift card)

```
Hi [Name],

I found your profile on [LinkedIn / industry directory] and thought you'd be the
right person to ask about this.

I'm building a product that gives SMB importers real-time visibility into their
international shipments — a small disposable label with a SIM that reports
location throughout the journey.

Before we build further, I need to talk to people like you who actually deal with
this daily. I'm looking for a 45-minute conversation about:
- How you track shipments today
- What frustrates you about the current process
- Whether our approach makes sense

It's a research conversation, not a sales call. I'll send a £30 Amazon gift card
as thanks for your time.

Interested? Here's my calendar: [Calendly link]

Best,
Denys Chumak
Founder, TIP (tip.live)
```

## Sourcing Channels Checklist

| Channel | Action | Target |
|---------|--------|--------|
| **LinkedIn Search** | "Procurement Manager" + "import" + company size 10-500 | 20+ profiles |
| **LinkedIn Groups** | Join supply chain / import-export groups, post research request | 5+ responses |
| **Industry events** | Check Freightos, Flexport, or local trade meetups for contacts | 3-5 leads |
| **Personal network** | Ask for warm intros to anyone who imports goods | 3-5 leads |
| **Respondent.io** | Post screener for consignee persona (~$100-150/interview) | Backup if needed |
| **UserInterviews.com** | Post screener for niche logistics roles | Backup if needed |
| **Reddit** | r/supplychain, r/logistics, r/freight — post research request | 2-3 leads |
| **Dec 2025 interviewees** | Re-contact the 8 people from customer discovery for referrals | 3-5 new leads |

# Interview Script — Forwarder (Freight Forwarder / Operations)

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Target:** 4-6 interviews
**Focus:** Workflow integration, activation experience, value to their clients

---

## Before the Call

- [ ] Confirm recording consent was given in scheduling email
- [ ] Have TIP activation flow mockup ready (QR scan → dashboard)
- [ ] Open this script + note-taking template
- [ ] Test Zoom/recording

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)

> "Thanks for taking the time. I'm Denys, and I'm building a product that helps cargo buyers track international shipments. I'm talking to forwarders because you're at the centre of the shipment journey, and I need to understand your perspective. No right or wrong answers — honest feedback is the most valuable thing. Everything stays confidential. OK to record?"

- Confirm recording consent
- "Could you tell me your name, role, and a bit about your company?"

---

## 2. Background & Context (5 min)

- What does your day-to-day look like?
- What types of cargo and trade lanes do you handle most?
- Roughly how many shipments per month does your team manage?
- What's the split between ocean, air, and road?

**Probes:**
- How many clients do you serve concurrently?
- What's your team size for operations?

---

## 3. Current Visibility & Client Communication (15 min)

> "I want to understand how you keep your clients informed about where their cargo is."

- How do you track shipments internally? What tools do you use?
- How do you share updates with clients? (email, portal, WhatsApp, phone)
- How often do clients ask you "where is my shipment?"
- What do you do when you don't have an answer?
- Do any of your clients currently use their own tracking devices or labels?

**Probes:**
- What percentage of your time goes into status updates vs actual logistics work?
- Have you ever lost a client because of poor visibility or communication?
- Do you offer any tracking portal or is it manual updates?
- Do your carriers give you reliable tracking data?

---

## 4. Pain Points & Problem Scenarios (10 min)

- What's the most frustrating part of keeping clients informed?
- Tell me about a time when a client was upset about lack of visibility. What happened?
- Where in the journey do you have the least visibility?
- Do you ever get blamed for delays you couldn't see coming?

**Probes:**
- How does poor visibility affect your relationship with clients?
- What would change if you could see every shipment on a map in real time?
- Is there a competitive pressure to offer better tracking?

---

## 5. Activation Workflow & Solution Fit (10 min)

> "Let me show you a concept and walk through how it would work at your end."

**[Share screen — show TIP label, QR scan activation flow, dashboard]**

> "The idea: your client buys a small label with a SIM inside. Before the shipment leaves origin, someone sticks it on the cargo and scans a QR code to activate it. From that point, both you and the client see the shipment on a live dashboard."

- Who at your end would physically attach and activate the label?
- Could that person do it in under 2 minutes?
- Would this fit into your existing warehouse or pickup workflow?
- Would you want access to the tracking dashboard too, or is this purely for the client?
- Would you recommend this to your clients?

**Probes:**
- What if the client sends you the label and asks you to activate it — is that a hassle or a service you'd offer?
- Would you charge clients extra for this, or include it in your service?
- Does city-level accuracy (not street-level) still have value for you?
- What would make you NOT want to deal with this?

---

## 6. Business Model & Partnership (5 min)

- If your clients started asking for this, would you want to be the one to sell/provide it?
- Would you prefer to resell labels at a markup, or just handle activation as a service?
- Would better tracking help you win new clients?
- How many of your clients do you think would use this?

**Probes:**
- What if TIP offered you a partner portal — bulk label management, client dashboards — would that be attractive?
- Would you trial this with one client to start?

---

## 7. Wrap-Up (5 min)

- Anything I should know that I didn't ask?
- Would you be interested in a pilot — testing this with one real shipment?
- Can you introduce me to any of your clients who might want to talk about their tracking pain?

> "This was really helpful. I'll send a thank-you within 24 hours. Feel free to reach out if anything comes to mind later."

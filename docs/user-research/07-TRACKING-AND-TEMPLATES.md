### Status values
- **Sourced** — identified, not yet contacted
- **Contacted** — outreach sent
- **Scheduled** — call booked
- **Completed** — interview done
- **No-show** — didn't attend
- **Declined** — not interested

### Columns to track
- **Source**: LinkedIn, referral, cold email, Respondent.io, personal network, Dec 2025 batch
- **Gift Card**: Pending / Sent / N/A
- **Pilot Interest**: 1-5 scale (asked at end of every interview)
- **Notes Link**: link to the post-interview summary for this participant

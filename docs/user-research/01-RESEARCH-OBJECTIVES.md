# TIP User Research — Objectives & Hypotheses

## Research Goal

Validate (or invalidate) TIP's core product assumptions by speaking directly with 15-20 people across three personas before committing to full-scale development.

---

## Key Hypotheses to Validate

| # | Hypothesis | How We'll Test | Success Signal |
|---|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| H1 | SMB importers lose money because they can't see where cargo is in real time | Consignee interviews — quantify frequency & cost of blind spots | 6+ of 10 report financial impact >$500/incident |
| H2 | Existing tracking tools (carrier portals, Excel, WhatsApp) are fragmented and unreliable | All personas — map current tool stack and satisfaction | Majority use 3+ tools and rate satisfaction ≤3/5 |
| H3 | A $20-25 disposable label is an acceptable price point for consignees | Consignee interviews — pricing section | 5+ of 10 say "yes" or "probably" at $20-25 |
| H4 | Forwarders would activate labels on behalf of their clients if the process takes <2 min | Forwarder interviews — activation flow walkthrough | 4+ of 6 say the workflow fits their process |
| H5 | Cell-tower-level accuracy (city/district) is "good enough" vs GPS precision | All personas — show accuracy example | Majority accept city-level for the price |
| H6 | The buyer (consignee) — not the shipper — is the right person to purchase | All personas — who pays, who decides | Clear pattern on purchasing authority |

---

## Research Questions by Theme

### 1. Problem Validation
- Do you experience cargo visibility gaps during international shipments?
- How often does a shipment arrive late or damaged without advance warning?
- What's the financial cost when you lose visibility? (delay penalties, customer churn, emergency freight)
- How do your own customers react when you can't tell them where their order is?

### 2. Current Solutions & Workarounds
- Walk me through how you track a shipment today, step by step.
- What tools or services do you use? (carrier portal, forwarder updates, WhatsApp, spreadsheets)
- How much do you pay for tracking today (directly or bundled)?
- What's good and what's broken about your current setup?

### 3. Value Proposition & Pricing
- [Show TIP concept] What's your first reaction?
- Would this solve the problem you described?
- What's missing that would make this a must-have?
- Is $20-25 per label per shipment a reasonable price? What would be too much?
- Who in your organisation would approve this purchase?

### 4. User Journey & Adoption
- Who physically handles cargo at origin — you, your forwarder, the factory?
- Could you (or your forwarder) stick a label on a box and scan a QR code in <2 minutes?
- What would block you from trying this on your next shipment?
- Would you want your customers to see the tracking dashboard too?

## Success Metrics for the Research Phase

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Interviews completed | 15-20 |
| Persona coverage | All 3 (Consignee ≥8, Forwarder ≥4, Shipper ≥2) |
| Hypotheses with clear signal | ≥4 of 6 validated or invalidated |
| Actionable insights documented | ≥10 unique insights with supporting quotes |
| Pilot candidates identified | ≥3 willing to join a paid pilot |

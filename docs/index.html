Vision 2030

Our Vision: Become the global standard for transparent cargo tracking, eliminating "black holes" in logistics. By 2030, enable real-time visibility for any shipment, anywhere, with predictive intelligence for delays and route deviations.

Problem & Solution

Problem: Black Holes

Cargo goes dark during transit (flights, ocean, remote areas) with no visibility

Problem: Fragmentation

Different carriers = different tracking systems, no unified view for customers

Problem: Reactive

Users discover delays after they happen, not before‚Äîcan't plan ahead

Our Solution

Offline data storage + single interface + proactive alerts = complete visibility

Full User Journey

1

Buy Label Consignee

Consignee (buyer) purchases label from website

2

Share Link Consignee

Consignee sends link to shipper/forwarder ‚Ä¢ 1-3 email reminders until activated ‚Ä¢ Link shows current label location

3

Enter Origin Shipper

Shipper clicks link, enters their address (cargo origin)

4

Fulfillment (Link SKU) HyperLabel

HyperLabel warehouse scans label ‚Üí Label ID linked to Order ‚Üí Ships to shipper (DHL/FedEx) ‚Üí Consignee notified

5

Receive Label Shipper

Shipper receives physical label with quick-start guide

6

Scan QR & Create Shipment Shipper ‚Äî MANDATORY

Shipper scans QR ‚Üí Creates Shipment record ‚Üí Links THIS label to THIS cargo ‚Ä¢ For bulk orders: scan each label separately (10 labels = 10 shipments)

7

Activate & Attach Shipper

Activate label (pull tab) ‚Ä¢ Enter final destination ‚Ä¢ Add cargo photo(s) ‚Ä¢ Attach label to cargo

8

Transit & Track Consignee

Cargo moves through carriers, label transmits every 120 min ‚Ä¢ Consignee tracks in real-time

9

Get Cargo Consignee

Cargo arrives at consignee, notification sent, tracking complete

Key Insight: The Consignee (buyer) may not know the shipper's exact address. They share a link so the shipper/forwarder can enter their own origin address. This triggers label shipment to the shipper.
